Settings for Youtube Embed Plug-in

This plug-in embeds a Youtube video specified by its link into a Blank Space field in the record details.

Select the field that holds the link to the Youtube video. Can be a Link field (URL) or Text field.

Select a Blank Space field to place the embedded video.

Aspect ratio lock (16:9):

Insert the desired width of the embedded video in pixels.

Insert the desired height of the embedded video in pixels.
